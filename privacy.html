口袋进销存 隐私政策

1. 数据收集与存储

1.1 主动输入的业务数据：您在使用本应用过程中输入的商品名称、库存数量、出入库记录、价格信息等数据，仅用于实现进销存核心功能，全部存储在您的移动设备本地，不会上传至本应用服务器、第三方平台或云端存储服务。

1.2 敏感信息：本应用会收集您的基础业务相关信息及设备基础信息，所有收集的信息仅存储在您的手机本地，不会上传至任何服务器，也不会泄露给任何第三方。

2.1 广告相关：应用内可能展示第三方广告，广告加载过程中可能获取设备唯一标识符（如OAID/AAID），仅用于广告投放与归因，不会关联您的业务数据，您可通过手机设置关闭广告追踪，也可选择观看广告获取应用增值服务，广告展示不影响核心功能使用。

2.2 通讯录权限：仅在您主动触发“从通讯录新增客户”功能时申请，权限仅用于读取您设备本地通讯录中的姓名、手机号信息，帮助您快速录入客户资料，所有通讯录数据仅存储在本地，不会上传至服务器或分享给第三方。

2.3 蓝牙权限：用于连接蓝牙打印机实现小票打印功能，仅在您主动操作打印时启用，权限仅用于扫描、配对周边蓝牙打印机设备，不会收集蓝牙设备的MAC地址等敏感信息，也不会用于定位或其他非打印相关用途。

2.4 位置权限：因Android系统机制限制，蓝牙设备扫描需获取位置权限（无需实际定位），该权限仅用于满足系统蓝牙扫描的权限校验要求，应用不会获取、存储或使用您的地理位置信息，也不会将位置相关数据分享给任何第三方。

2.5 摄像头权限：仅在您主动使用“扫描条形码”功能时申请，用于识别商品、订单的条形码/二维码，快速查找对应信息，权限使用过程中仅临时调用摄像头画面，不会拍摄、存储照片或视频，也不会上传相关数据。

2.6 权限自主控制：所有权限均为可选权限，您可在手机“设置-应用-口袋进销存-权限”中随时开启/关闭，权限关闭仅影响对应功能使用，不影响应用核心进销存功能的正常运行。

3.1 数据使用范围：本地存储的业务数据仅用于您本人查看、编辑、导出库存信息，应用开发者无法查看、获取或修改您的任何本地数据。

3.2 数据删除：您卸载本应用后，设备内存储的所有应用相关数据将被自动彻底删除，且无法恢复。

3.3 数据备份：应用不提供自动备份功能，如需备份数据，请手动导出至设备本地文件夹。

Pocket Inventory - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 9, 2026

1. Data Collection and Storage

1.1 Voluntarily Entered Business Data: Product names, inventory quantities, stock in/out records, price information and other data you enter while using the App are only used to implement core inventory management functions. All data is stored locally on your mobile device and will not be uploaded to the App's servers, third-party platforms, or cloud storage services.

1.2 Sensitive Information: The App collects your basic business-related information and device basic information. All collected information is stored locally on your mobile device only, will not be uploaded to any server, and will not be disclosed to any third party.

2. Permission Usage Instructions

2.1 Advertising-related: Third-party advertisements may be displayed in the App. During ad loading, the App may obtain device unique identifiers (such as OAID/AAID) only for ad delivery and attribution, without associating with your business data. You can turn off ad tracking through mobile settings, or choose to watch ads to get value-added services of the App. Ad display does not affect the use of core functions.

2.2 Contacts Permission: Applied only when you actively trigger the "Add Customers from Contacts" function. This permission is only used to read name and phone number information from your local device contacts to help you quickly enter customer information. All contact data is stored locally only and will not be uploaded to servers or shared with third parties.

2.3 Bluetooth Permission: Used to connect to Bluetooth printers for receipt printing. It is only enabled when you actively operate printing. This permission is only used to scan and pair surrounding Bluetooth printer devices, will not collect sensitive information such as MAC addresses of Bluetooth devices, and will not be used for positioning or other non-printing related purposes.

2.4 Location Permission: Due to Android system mechanism restrictions, Bluetooth device scanning requires location permission (no actual positioning required). This permission is only used to meet the permission verification requirements of system Bluetooth scanning. The App will not obtain, store or use your geographic location information, nor will it share location-related data with any third party.

2.5 Camera Permission: Applied only when you actively use the "Scan Barcode" function to identify barcodes/QR codes of products and orders for quick lookup of corresponding information. During the use of this permission, only the camera screen is temporarily called, no photos or videos are taken or stored, and no related data is uploaded.

2.6 Independent Permission Control: All permissions are optional. You can enable/disable them at any time in "Settings - Apps - Pocket Inventory - Permissions" on your mobile phone. Disabling permissions only affects the use of corresponding functions and does not affect the normal operation of the App's core inventory management functions.

3. Data Usage and Control Rights

3.1 Scope of Data Usage: Locally stored business data is only used for you to view, edit, and export inventory information. The App developer cannot view, access, or modify any of your local data.

3.2 Data Deletion: All application-related data stored on your device will be automatically and completely deleted and cannot be recovered after you uninstall the App.

3.3 Data Backup: The App does not provide automatic backup functionality. If you need to back up data, please manually export it to the local folder of your device.

4. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in accordance with business adjustments or regulatory requirements. The updated policy will be announced in the App, and your continued use of the App constitutes your agreement to the updated policy.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us via email at z136759790@gmail.com or leave a message in the App comment section. We will reply within 7 working days.
