Fedis 隐私政策

生效时间：2026年3月5日

1. 数据收集与存储

1.1 Redis 连接与数据：您在使用本应用过程中输入的 Redis 服务器地址、端口、密码等连接信息，以及从 Redis 实例读取的键值数据，仅用于实现 Redis 可视化查看和管理功能，全部存储在您的移动设备本地，不会上传至本应用服务器、第三方平台或云端存储服务。

1.2 设备基础信息（可选）：若您后续选择开启广告服务，应用可能获取设备广告标识符（AAID），该信息仅用于个性化广告推荐（本地计算），不会传输至任何服务器；若未开启广告服务，应用不会获取任何设备标识信息。

1.3 敏感信息：本应用不会收集、存储您的手机号、身份证号、银行卡信息、地理位置、通讯录、照片等敏感个人信息。

2. 数据使用与控制权

2.1 数据使用范围：本地存储的 Redis 连接信息和缓存数据仅用于您本人查看、编辑、管理 Redis 实例，应用开发者无法查看、获取或修改您的任何本地数据及 Redis 服务器数据。

2.2 数据删除：您可通过应用内「设置-清除连接记录」功能删除所有本地存储的 Redis 连接信息，删除操作不可逆，数据删除后无法恢复；Redis 服务器数据的修改/删除由您自行操作，应用仅提供操作入口，不承担数据丢失责任。

2.3 数据备份：应用不提供 Redis 数据自动备份功能，如需备份 Redis 数据，请手动通过应用导出至设备本地文件夹。

3. 广告服务（可选）

为支撑应用持续维护与更新，本应用后续可能添加非干扰性广告服务（如原生广告、横幅广告）：

3.1 广告服务商：Google AdMob（谷歌）；

3.2 数据收集：广告服务商可能通过应用获取设备广告标识符（AAID），用于提供个性化广告推荐，遵循 Google 隐私政策；

3.3 选择权：您可通过设备系统设置关闭个性化广告推荐，或通过应用内功能关闭广告展示（若提供付费去广告服务）。

4. 隐私政策的更新

我们可能会根据业务调整或法规要求更新本隐私政策，更新后的政策将在应用内公示，继续使用应用即表示您同意更新后的政策。

5. 联系我们

若您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，可通过应用内「设置-帮助中心」提交反馈，我们将在7个工作日内回复。

Fedis - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 5, 2026

1. Data Collection and Storage

1.1 Redis Connection and Data: Redis server address, port, password and other connection information you enter while using the App, as well as key-value data read from Redis instances, are only used to implement Redis visualization and management functions. All data is stored locally on your mobile device and will not be uploaded to the App's servers, third-party platforms, or cloud storage services.

1.2 Device Basic Information (Optional): If you choose to enable ad services in the future, the App may collect the Advertising ID (AAID) of your device. This information is only used for personalized ad recommendations (local calculation) and will not be transmitted to any server; if ad services are not enabled, the App will not collect any device identification information.

1.3 Sensitive Information: The App will not collect or store your sensitive personal information such as mobile phone number, ID card number, bank card information, geographic location, contacts, photos, etc.

2. Data Usage and Control Rights

2.1 Scope of Data Usage: Locally stored Redis connection information and cached data are only used for you to view, edit, and manage Redis instances. The App developer cannot view, access, or modify any of your local data or Redis server data.

2.2 Data Deletion: You can delete all locally stored Redis connection information through the "Settings - Clear Connection Records" function in the App. The deletion operation is irreversible, and data cannot be recovered after deletion; modification/deletion of Redis server data is operated by you, the App only provides an operation entry and is not responsible for data loss.

2.3 Data Backup: The App does not provide automatic backup functionality for Redis data. If you need to back up Redis data, please manually export it to the local folder of your device through the App.

3. Ad Services (Optional)

To support the continuous maintenance and update of the App, non-intrusive ad services (such as native ads, banner ads) may be added in the future:

3.1 Ad Service Provider: Google AdMob (Google);

3.2 Data Collection: The ad service provider may collect the device's Advertising ID (AAID) through the App to provide personalized ad recommendations, in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy;

3.3 Right of Choice: You can turn off personalized ad recommendations through your device's system settings, or disable ad display through in-App functions (if a paid ad-free service is provided).

4. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in accordance with business adjustments or regulatory requirements. The updated policy will be announced in the App, and your continued use of the App constitutes your agreement to the updated policy.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can submit feedback through "Settings - Help Center" in the App, and we will reply within 7 working days.
